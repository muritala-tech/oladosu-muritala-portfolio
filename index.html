About Me

Hi, I'm Oladosu Muritala Senior UI/UX Designer | Problem Solver | Storyteller I don’t just design screens; I design solutions. With a background spanning Fintech, Edtech, Healthcare, Ecommerce, Real Estate, and Extended Reality (XR), I’ve spent my career turning complex technical hurdles into seamless, human-centered digital experiences. My Philosophy I believe that the best design is invisible. If a user has to ask for help, the design hasn't finished its job yet. My mission is to create products that are so intuitive and usable that they feel like second nature. I’m obsessed with the "micro-details"—the small interactions and thoughtful touches that turn a casual user into a loyal customer.
